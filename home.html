My favourite Sport

Football

Football, or association football (commonly known as soccer), is the world's most popular sport, featuring two teams of 11 players trying to score by moving a spherical ball into the opposing goal. Played on a rectangular field, it requires using any part of the body except arms and hands, with the goalkeeper being the sole exception within their penalty area.
