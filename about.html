My favourite Singer

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh is an Indian playback singer, composer, music producer and instrumentalist. A leading figure in contemporary Hindi film music, he is the recipient of several accolades including two National Film Awards and eight Filmfare Awards. He was conferred the Padma Shri by the Government of India in 2025
