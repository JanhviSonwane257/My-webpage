Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian thin, crispy or soft crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and black gram (urad dal). Known for being a nutritious breakfast, it is high in carbohydrates and easy to digest. Served hot, it is often paired with chutney and sambar, with variations like masala dosa and set dosa.
